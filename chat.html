Title: Nigeria's AI Challenge: Turning Potential into Progress

The global race for AI supremacy is reshaping economic power, with the US and China leading the charge, while Nigeria faces challenges in keeping pace. AI is projected to contribute $4.4 trillion to the global economy by 2030, but Nigeria risks being left behind due to inadequate infrastructure and political commitment. With over 60% of its population under 25, Nigeria has immense potential to harness AI, but it must invest in energy, education, and infrastructure, and develop a national AI strategy to avoid economic marginalization. By embracing bold policies and fostering innovation, Nigeria can transform its challenges into opportunities and secure a competitive position in the AI-driven future.
